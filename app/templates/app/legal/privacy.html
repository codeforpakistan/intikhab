How we protect and handle your information

1. Information We Collect

On the Intikhab platform, we collect various types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Identity Information: Age, location, nationality (for voter verification)
  • Voting Data: Your encrypted votes and election participation
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser details, usage patterns (for security)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide secure voting services
  • Verify your identity and voting eligibility
  • Ensure election integrity and prevent fraud
  • Improve the platform security and functionality
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When legally required by court order
  • For security and fraud prevention purposes
  • With trusted service providers (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Vote Secrecy

Your vote is completely secret and anonymous:

  • Votes are encrypted and cannot be traced back to individual voters
  • We use advanced cryptographic techniques to ensure vote privacy
  • Only aggregated, anonymous results are made public
  • No one, including system administrators, can see how you voted

5. Data Security

We use state-of-the-art security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure, encrypted databases
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Homomorphic encryption for vote counting

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to improve your experience and ensure security. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser, but this may affect some platform functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Download your data (excluding vote records for privacy)
  • Delete your account (after elections are complete)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Age Requirements

This platform is for eligible voters aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely in Pakistan. If necessary, we may transfer data internationally with appropriate safeguards and in compliance with Pakistani data protection laws.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email and platform notifications.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us.
